About Company

Kazakhmys Coal

Kazakhmys Coal is a production company engaged in mining and supplying thermal coal from the "Molodezhny" open-pit mine located at the Borly coal deposit in the Karaganda Region.

The company is part of Kazakhmys Holding and is a key supplier of coal products for regional electricity generation.

  • Full production cycle
  • Design capacity — 10 million tons per year
  • Long-term B2B contracts
