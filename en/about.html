About Company

Kazakhmys Coal

A production company engaged in mining and supplying thermal coal for power generation enterprises in the Republic of Kazakhstan and beyond.

General Information

Kazakhmys Coal

Kazakhmys Coal carries out mining and supply of thermal coal from the “Molodezhny” open-pit mine located at the Borly coal deposit in the Karaganda Region.

The company is part of the thermal power complex of Kazakhmys Holding LLP and is a key regional supplier of thermal coal.

The main consumers are power plants and heat supply enterprises of Kazakhstan, as well as selected consumers in the Russian Federation.
